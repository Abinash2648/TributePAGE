About the Hitman

☛ Rohit sharma, in full Rohit Gurunath Sharma, was born on October 30, 1987, in Bansod, Maharshatra, India.

☛ he current captain of India men’s cricket team in all formats. Considered one of the best batsmen of his generation and one of greatest opening batters of all time,[3] Sharma is known for his timing, elegance, six-hitting abilities and leadership skills.

☛ Sharma currently holds the world record for the highest individual score (264) in a One Day International (ODI) match and is the only player to have scored three double-centuries in ODIs and also holds the record for scoring most hundreds (five) in a single Cricket World Cup, for which he won the ICC Men's ODI Cricketer of the Year award in 2019. .

☛ In November 2013, during Sachin Tendulkar's farewell series, Sharma made his Test debut at Eden Gardens in Kolkata against West Indies and scored 177

☛Sharma joined the Indian Premier League (IPL) in 2008 when he was signed by the Deccan Chargers franchise, based in Hyderabad, for the sum of US$750,000 a year.[82] In the 2011 auction, he was sold for US$2 million to the Mumbai Indians.[83] He scored his only IPL century in the 2012 tournament with 109 (not out) against the Kolkata Knight Riders.[84] Under his leadership, Mumbai has won the IPL in 2013, 2015, 2017, 2019 and 2020; they also won the former Champions League Twenty20 competition in 2013.[85]

☛ Sharma has been sponsored by several brands including CEAT and the Swiss watchmaker Hublot.[117][118] In his career, Sharma has endorsed many other brands including Maggi, Fair and Lovely, Lay's, Nissan, energy drink Relentless, Nasivion nasal spray, Aristocrat by VIP Industries, Adidas and Oppo mobiles.

☛Rohit Sharma has hit four centuries in T20Is, the most for any batter in the shortest form of the game.
☛Rohit Sharma is the leading run-scorer in the world in T20Is with 3737 runs.
☛Rohit has hit five centuries in the 2019 World Cup which is the most by any batter in a single tournament/series.
☛Rohit is the most capped player in T20Is overall, having appeared in 142 matches for India.
